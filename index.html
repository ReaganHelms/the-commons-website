Weekend Services

Sundays at 10:00 AM

New Community Church is a community of practice dedicated to learning and embodying the way of Jesus. Our Sunday gatherings are designed to be welcoming for everyone, whether you're new to faith or have been walking with Jesus for years.

Join us as we worship together, learn from God's Word, and build meaningful relationships. Come as you are and experience authentic community centered on practicing Jesus' teachings. We also offer livestream services and programs for kids and youth.

Ascend Coffee Roasters

Open Daily: 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Experience exceptional, ethically sourced coffee roasted right here in Vista. Ascend Coffee Roasters is dedicated to finding the right bean for every roast profile, with trained baristas extracting the perfect espresso every time.

Beyond craft coffee, we offer fresh breakfast and lunch items made to order. Whether you need your morning fuel, a quick bite, or want to connect with friends, our coffee bar brings our community together with quality and warm hospitality.
