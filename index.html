The Relationship Resource: Counseling Center

Our professional counseling center provides compassionate, faith-based counseling services to individuals, couples, and families in our community. It serves as a space to provide resources and hold education in a community setting to empower the community to be proactive in healing and restoration.

We believe in the power of healing and restoration through professional counseling combined with biblical principles. Our counselors are here to walk alongside you on your journey toward wholeness.

About The Commons

Designed as an evolving space to be a place for the community.

Redesigned from a building that served as a Walgreens for decades. Located at the entrance of Historic Vista, The Commons is seated at the footstep of the Vista arch.

The Commons is founded on cultivating a consistent presence for our community through a wide range of community-oriented events, partnerships, and collaborations.

The Commons is a community-driven environment rooted in the common good of loving neighbors and serving the community.

Our vision is to be a hub for worship, community engagement, and authentic connection. From weekend gatherings to coffee and counseling, we're here to love our community with shared purpose.

ASL Interpretation

We provide ASL interpretation for our Sunday services both in-person and online. Our goal is to make worship accessible to everyone in our community, ensuring that all can participate fully in our gatherings.

In addition to in-person interpretation, we offer a live ASL stream on our dedicated Deaf Ministry Facebook page. For more information about our deaf ministry services or to connect with our ASL interpreters, please reach out to Roy.
