Ascend Coffee Roasters

Open Daily: 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM

ASL Interpretation

We provide ASL interpretation for our Sunday services both in-person and online. Our goal is to make worship accessible to everyone in our community, ensuring that all can participate fully in our gatherings.

In addition to in-person interpretation, we offer a live ASL stream on our dedicated Deaf Ministry Facebook page. For more information about our deaf ministry services or to connect with our ASL interpreters, please reach out to Roy.
